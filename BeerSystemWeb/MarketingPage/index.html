About FreshBeerNearMe

Fresh Beer Near Me is a mobile beer discovery application which is created to allow beer drinkers to have a better understanding and knowledge about beer.

By helping beer drinkers to gain deeper insights to beers, Fresh Beer Near Me partnered with Binary Beer, utilising their Smart Keg tracking and management technology to showcase to beer drinkers a comprehensive beer discovery experience to provide more information such as beer freshness, beers on tap, temperature and tasting notes.

Other than having information on beer, Fresh Beer Near Me also provides beer drinkers to keep track of the venues that they patronise and also to discover new beers and breweries that are near them as well. Beer drinkers can leave reviews or ratings of the beer that they experienced and also the venues that they went to.

FreshBeerNearMe Subscription Plan $74.99 per month

As we are a subscription-based pricing model, we decide to charge $74.99 per month to our non-Binary Beer customers. New beer owners will get a free trial of 3 months to try out our software before deciding whether they want to continue using our services.

Our Binary Beer customers get to enjoy daily updates of freshness and temperature of their kegs and they will be allowed to use our software for free.

Find out more on why you should use our software by looking at the features that we provide below.

Features

Publish & Advertise your pub

By signing up with us, you get to publish your pub in our application. This means that our beer drinkers will be able to locate your pub through the app.

Customize your menu

Our application allows you to customize your own menu through our web application. Your customers can now have a easier time viewing whats on your menu.

Daily Updates on freshness & temperature

As a binary beer customer, you get to enjoy automatic daily updates on your kegs. This will ensure that you will always be up-to-date with the condition of your beers. Rest assured if you aren't a binary beer customer, you get to update your kegs through the web application.

Rating & Review

Watch how your venue/beer is doing with our rating and review. With this, you can make use of the feedbacks to better your establishment.

Promotion

Create exclusive promotions with our application to attract your customers.

Ranking

See how well your venue is doing against other competitors.
